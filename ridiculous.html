The Most Ridiculous Rule

No Pencil!

The most ridiculous rule that I have had was in 5th grade. We were required to write in pen, not pencil. If you didn't have a pen and you wrote in pencil, you would get points off.

Cursive Only!

We were also required to write in cursive, not print. The same punishment applies. If you didn't write in cursive, you would get points off.
